Let’s Strengthen This Movement

by Arvind Kejriwal   

It is no secret that the results of the recently completed elections in Punjab, Goa and Delhi were not to our expectations. As an ambitious upstart in the dirty world of Indian politics, the Aam Aadmi Party – your party – has always aimed high.

But in the long arc of history, this is just a blip. We have to remind ourselves how far we have come from where we began. In the last 2 years alone, we have formed a government in Delhi with a historic mandate of 67 out of 70, we have won 22 Assembly seats and become the Opposition party in Punjab, and we now have 49 councillors in Delhi’s municipal corporations.

This also means that we have a responsibility of delivering on the dreams and aspirations of crores of people and we are focused on doing exactly that. We will learn from our mistakes, we will redraw our plans and we will move forward to strengthen this movement.

But the last few weeks of unsparing attacks, unsubstantiated allegations and institutional witch-hunting of AAP reminds what exactly we are battling against. We are fighting a world where public institutions are used as pawns to achieve political ends and where a deeply biased media isn’t interested in uncovering the truth.

But that won’t deter us from following our path. Beginning with this monthly newsletter series, we will communicate regularly and directly with you and we promise to listen carefully to what you have to say. So as we march forward, we ask you to join us and do your bit in strengthening this movement.
